Tips to follow when working out


Be sure to hydrate throughout the day. It takes a couple

of hours for your body to absorb water


Both cardio and strength workouts require protein

I recommend whey or soy protein shakes.


Carbs are our body’s main source of fuel,

yogurt, protein shakes; include a banana = high glycemic carbohydrates

Pick a cardio exercise: running, walking, swimming, biking, hiking, rowing, stairmaster,


Stretching after cardio prepares body for moderate exercise

Work the muscles until they're tired, if you work five sets, the last two or three less intense


Limit your workouts to 30-90 minutes, work at a lower intensity, the following week:
recovery = 12 to 14 days.

If you get tired feet rub Noxzema on the bottom and sides.
